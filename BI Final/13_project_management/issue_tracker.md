I use this tracker to record unresolved preparation, documentation, data quality, Excel, Power BI, or report issues. I do not treat an issue as a finding; I treat it as something to resolve or explain before submission.

## Priority Guide

- **High:** The issue blocks analysis or submission.
- **Medium:** The issue requires documentation or a methodological decision.
- **Low:** The issue is cosmetic or organizational.
